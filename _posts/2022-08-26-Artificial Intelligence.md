Artificial Intelligence (AI) objective is to build an intelligent agents. AI consists of Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision (CV), Neural Network (NN), Deep Learning (DL), Natural Langauge Processing (NLP) and others. The relation between AI,ML, and DL is shown in the below figure.

Machine Learning gives the ability machine to learn without explicitly being programmed. The ML paradigm is mainly divided into three categories - 1) Supervised Learning 2) Unsupervised Learning 3) Reinforcement Learning 4) Semi-Supervised Learning and 5) Self Supervised Learning.
