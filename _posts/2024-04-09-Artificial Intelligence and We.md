In the last two decades, we are using the Artificial Intelligence (AI) in various corner of our lives, from smartwatches to space programs. Today *AI has permeated our daily lives, and today, we are very near to relying on it*. What is AI? There is no universal definition exists which defines it. However, according to emeritus Stanford Prof. John McCarthy, who coined the term AI in 1955 defined it as <u>"science and engineering of making intelligent machines”</u>.


In the present day, you may have encountered terms like machine learning, computer vision, deep learning, natural language processing, among others. These terms represent components of artificial intelligence (AI), which encompasses machine learning (ML), computer vision (CV), neural networks (NN), deep learning (DL), natural language processing (NLP), and various other fields.
