Research Interests I have broad interests in knowledge graphs and machine learning (in particular deep learning) for application into natural language processing, recommender systems, and computational social data science. My research is mostly directed towards: Knowledge-infused Learning: is a class of Neuro-Symbolic AI techniques that utilize a variety of knowledge (lexical, linguistic, domain specific, commonsense, process knowledge and constraint-based) in different forms and abstractions into deep neural network. It improves upon data-centric statistical learning to reduce training, reduce computing needs, and broaden coverage, resulting in improved performance, safety and model interpretation, and providing user-level explanations. Knowledge Intensive Language Understanding: is a new unified benchmark to help AI researchers build models that are better able to leverage real-world knowledge to accomplish a broad range of tasks.
